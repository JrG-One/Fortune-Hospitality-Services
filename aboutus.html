    Fortune Hospitality Services

At the outset, we take this opportunity to introduce ourselves as one of the leading service providers in the field of Catering, Housekeeping, and other Allied Services.
FHS is a Partnership Firm with Partners named Mr. Harish V. Shetty & Dayanand V. Shetty, who have a rich & meaningful experience of 25 years in the field of Hospitality Services.
We are at the forefront of providing complete Catering Services, Housekeeping Mgt., Laundry, Plant & Event Management. Additionally, FHS has expanded its line of services to include facility management.
Our professionally managed group has been providing the best quality, with high standards and customer-satisfactory services. We have also been providing safety and site management guidelines for all our Valued Clients. Most of our Services are cost-effective, health-friendly, and designed in such a way that they would suit the requirements of all our valued customers.
We have acquired the best human assets to provide all possible types of services at any place at any given point of time. We have also been practicing modern methodology and using scientific and modernized equipment for cooking. Our main motto is to grow with an organization wherever associated and if given a chance we will endeavor to put our best & sincere efforts to prove our capabilities and competencies and fulfill our commitments.
